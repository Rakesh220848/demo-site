What is MIRROR-VERSE?

MIRROR-VERSE is an immersive treasure hunt experience where reality meets reflection. As part of IGNITE 2024, this event challenges teams to navigate through a series of cryptic clues, mirrored messages, and mind-bending puzzles scattered across the campus.

Every clue is a doorway to another dimension. Every reflection hides a secret. Teams must decode encrypted messages, solve riddles, and think in reverse to progress through the hunt.

üí° The Concept: In the Mirror-Verse, nothing is straightforward. Text appears reversed, numbers hide letters, and the path forward often requires looking backward. Only those who can see beyond the obvious will claim victory.

How to Play

Game Flow

Step 1

Registration & Check-In

Register your team online and report to the starting point 15 minutes before the event. You'll receive your team ID and initial instructions.

Step 2

Receive First Clue

At the starting signal, each team receives their first cryptic clue. Decode it to discover your next location on campus.

Step 3

Navigate Checkpoints

Travel to each checkpoint marked on your clues. At each location, find the hidden marker and complete the challenge to unlock the next clue.

Step 4

Solve Mirror Puzzles

Each checkpoint features unique MIRROR-VERSE themed puzzles: reversed text, cipher codes, riddles, and reflection-based challenges.

Step 5

Reach the Final Destination

The final clue leads you to the treasure location. Be the first team to arrive and claim your victory!
